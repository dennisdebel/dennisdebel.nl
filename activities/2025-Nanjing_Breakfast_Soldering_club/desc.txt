透明社会 | 早餐与电路改造工作坊 | Breakfast And Circuit Bending Workshop电路弯曲（Circuit Bending）是一种创造性地误用的艺术。具体而言，就是对电子设备如发声玩具或日用电子产品的误用。在这次工作坊中，我们将前往南京的早市寻找合适的电子设备，之后我们将回到透明社会并对我们的发现进行改造和即兴创作！<br>
<br><br>
第一部分：参观早市，寻找一些能够发出光和声音的二手电子设备。<br>
你将学到：如何找到合适的设备？需要注意些什么？如何讨价还价？
<br><br>
第二部分：一起吃早饭。<br>
你将变得：比较健康。
<br><br>
第二部分：电路改造，将电子设备原本的功能转化为新的、有趣的、富有诗意或恼人的效果。<br>
你将学习的内容包括：如何拆解设备，如何重新组装设备，基本的工作原理，以及如何用简单的手段（手指/接触点、可变电阻器、跳线）来操作电子电路。
<br><br>
时间地点：<br>
10月5日早8点前往位于宝船公园旁的早市寻找二手电子设备。<br>
10月5日上午至中午在云南路附近的透明社会进行电路改造工作。<br>

Transparent Society | Breakfast and Circuit Bending Workshop | Circuit Bending Workshop Circuit bending is the art of creative misuse. Specifically, it involves misapplying electronic devices, such as sound-producing toys or everyday electronics. In this workshop, we'll visit Nanjing's morning markets to search for suitable electronic devices. Afterward, we'll return to Transparent Society to modify and improvise with our finds!
<br><br>
Part 1: Visit the morning market to find some used electronic devices that emit light and sound.
<br><br>
You'll learn: How to find the right devices? What to look out for? How to bargain?
<br><br>
Part 2: Eat breakfast together.<br>
You'll become: Healthier.<br>
<br><br>
Part 2: Circuit bending: Transforming the original functions of electronic devices into new, interesting, poetic, or even annoying effects.<br>
You'll learn: How to disassemble and reassemble devices, basic working principles, and how to manipulate electronic circuits using simple tools (fingers/touch points, variable resistors, jumper wires).
<br><br>
Time and Location:<br>
On October 5th, at 8:00 AM, head to the morning market near Baochuan Park to search for used electronic devices.<br>
From the morning to noon of October 5th, conduct circuit reconstruction work at the Transparent Society near Yunnan Road.
<br><br>
We are the Breakfast Welding Club:<br>
Dennis de Belle (NL) is an artist and educator living in Shanghai who seeks to escape screen time through (de)construction, welding, and cleaning up junk.<br>
Liu Chang (CN) enjoys BK's dismantling and believes that making friends with ghostly legacies can be a less capitalist way to express oneself.
