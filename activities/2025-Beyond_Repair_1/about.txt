Beyond Repair developed for <a href="https://www.chronusartcenter.org" target="blanko">Chronus Art Center</a> attempts to discuss issues surrounding repair cultures, consumerism, waste streams and recycling through a series of (self) organized hand-on workshops. <br>
Beyond Repair #1 focusses on the act of recycling, its ethics, economics and practicality. We start at the end of the repair cycle: trash. Or is it the beginning? For this even we will try to collectively collect trash and delivery it at a special recycling box that will reward us in cold hard digital cash. This is an experiment, not to make money, but to spend time nearby trash and recycling machines and people to discuss the economics and nature and culture around trash and recycling. <br><br>

The evening ended after collecting around 20kg of trash which gave us around 10RMB to buy ice cream for everyone.<br><br>


Beyond Repair #1 was organised with the help of <a href="https://parages.github.io/">Liu Chang</a>/Parages, at <a href="https://www.instagram.com/transparent.society.nanking/">Transparent Society</a> Nanjing, China, a self-organised space for self-organisation.<br>
