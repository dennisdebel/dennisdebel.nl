Experimental edible high speed network cable based on glass noodles. Leveraging the mediums properties for both light/data transportation as well as culinary delight.

SPA LW SA MM 6: SPECIAL APPLICATION LIGHT WEIGHT SINGLE ARMOR MULTIMODE AIR BLOWN FIBER
6 Minute cooking time. (EC) No 669/2009 annex I & (EU) No 187/2011 , 618/2013 annex I compliant. No patent pending.
